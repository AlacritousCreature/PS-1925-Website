About Us

PS-1925® is a drone manufacturer, technology and service provider And designs Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) and Founded by Pawan Kumar and Shefali Vinod Ramteke.

The motto of our company is to introduce Indigenious Drone technology in India, based on the principles of Make in India - Manufactured, Designed and Developed in India.

We are working on drones/UAVs for the past 4 years in our institution (IIIT Allahabad) and have developed Radio-Controlled Aircrafts and drones for different applications.

We are proposing “Desi Drones” for 7 major sectors of the Indian Economy:

  • Agriculture – Krishi PS-1925®
  • Disaster Management and Relief Operations – Rahat PS-1925®
  • Defence – Rakshak PS-1925®
  • E commerce – Courier PS-1925®
  • Health – Chikitsa PS-1925®
  • Solar – Surya PS-1925®
  • Transportation – Yatra PS-1925®
  • Cinematography – Drishyam PS-1925®
  • Delhivery – Vitaran PS-1925®
