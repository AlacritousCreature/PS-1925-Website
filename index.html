About Us

PS-1925® is a drone manufacturer, technology and service provider And designs Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) and Founded by Pawan Kumar and Shefali Vinod Ramteke.

The motto of our company is to introduce Indigenious Drone technology in India, based on the principles of Make in India - Manufactured, Designed and Developed in India.

We are working on drones/UAVs for the past 4 years in our institution (IIIT Allahabad) and have developed Radio-Controlled Aircrafts and drones for different applications.

We are proposing “Desi Drones” for 8 major sectors of the Indian Economy:

  • Agriculture – Krishi PS-1925®
  • Disaster Management and Relief Operations – Rahat PS-1925®
  • Defence – Rakshak PS-1925®
  • E commerce – Vitaran PS-1925®
  • Health – Chikitsa PS-1925®
  • Solar – Surya PS-1925®
  • Transportation – Yatra PS-1925®
  • Cinematography – Drishyam PS-1925®

Why KRISHI PS-1925?

Easy fold-up design

Decreases up to 90% of the space, wide-variety of customizable frames available.

Three flight modes

GPS mode, AB point mode, Manual mode/Auto Operation mode, according to terrain and other requirements.

Automatic shut down

of water pump and continuous spraying at the current point when certain signals are encountered.

Highly Efficient Spraying System

with 10L wave-proof tank, 4 nozzles, spray width of 3-5 m, operating efficiency of 3.5L/min. Custom spraying range and a speed controlled intelligent spraying flow.

The high-precision obstacle avoidance radar

works both during the day and night without being influenced by light or dust, easily detects cm-wide power lines from up to 15 m away and protects the aircraft from obstacles.

RTK

An advanced high precision navigation and positioning system, provides accurate 3D positioning. RTK makes the system resistant to magnetic interference allowing for accurate flight in areas where other aircraft cannot function./p>

Terrain Follower Radar

Scans the terrain and maintains a cm-accurate height above crops with a constant spray density.

Wide angle FPV camera and searchlight

for 24*7 monitoring and remote controller’s built-in display for avoiding obstacles in long distance operations.

Black box

Function supporting 100,000 times cycle read and write and recording capacity of 32-hour long flight to record flight data.
